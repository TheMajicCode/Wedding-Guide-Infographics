Your Wedding Planning Roadmap

12+ Months Out: Dream & Research

This is the blue-sky phase. Set your budget, draft a preliminary guest list, and begin researching venues and key vendors that match your vision.

9-11 Months Out: Book the Big Stuff

Time for major decisions. Secure your venue to lock in your date. Then, book high-priority vendors like your photographer, caterer, and entertainment.

6-8 Months Out: Details & Attire

Send out your save-the-dates to let guests know. Begin the fun part of shopping for your wedding attire and booking your florist.

3-5 Months Out: Finalize & Invite

The wedding is getting real! Order and mail your official invitations. Arrange for all rentals and conduct hair and makeup trials.

1-2 Months Out: The Nitty-Gritty

Finalize the details. Obtain your marriage license, create the seating chart, write your vows, and confirm all final arrangements with your vendors.

The Final Week: Relax & Delegate

Pack for your honeymoon! Give final payments and instructions to your vendors. Entrust day-of tasks to your wedding party and get ready to enjoy your day.
